PB&J

The peanut butter and jelly sandwich (affectionately called a PB&J) is a tasty American staple and makes a quick, easy lunch or snack. You can have it the traditional way or you can get creative put lots of fun, tasty treats in it.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Peanut Butter
  • Jelly

    • Directions:

    • 1. Spread peanut butter onto one slice of bread.
    • 2. Spread jelly onto other slice of bread.
    • 3. place slice of bread with peanut butter onto slice of bread with jelly.
